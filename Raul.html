Book-Magazine Design: Volume 2

About the Project

Volume 2 is the second edition of Raul Mourão’s book-magazine. Organized in chapters that address diverse readings, topics, and writings, the publication creates a dialogue between multiple languages of Brazilian and global contemporary art, including cinema and music.

Project Details

  • Project Title: Volume 2
  • Conception: Raul Mourão
  • Edition: Automatica Edições
  • Graphic Design: Gustavo Prado, Jonathan Nunes, Maria Clara Ruback
  • Image Editing: Thiago Barros
  • Final Artwork: Dínamo Design
