Raul Mourão
Volume 3

Volume 3 is the third edition of Raul Mourão's book-magazine. Organized in chapters that present the main records of the artist's exhibition journey between 2018 and 2022, the publication includes critical texts that provide an overview of his production in dialogue with aesthetic, social, and political issues. Following the previous editions, Volume 3 brings together multifaceted records of sculptures, paintings, drawings, photos, and video frames that composed the artist's major exhibitions over the last five years.
