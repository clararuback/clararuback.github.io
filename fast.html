Avoiding hotel queues with
TOTVS Fast Check-in

Redesigning a feature to improve guest experience and operational efficiency.

The Challenge

Brazilian legislation requires extensive information during check-in, which consumes significant time for hotel staff and guests, leading to long queues during peak periods.

The Solution

O TOTVS Fast Check-in was created to collect all mandatory data before the guest's arrival, allowing them to simply present their ID and proceed directly to their room.

Primary Issues

  • Usability and development flaws
  • Poor integration with the PMS (Property Management System)
  • Available only in the desktop version

Our Intervention

By analyzing user behavior via DataDog, we implemented several improvements to the flow and interface. After those fixes, we closely monitored the functionality, engaging the team to work with data.

Results

+400%

Usage (Fev/24 vs Fev/23)

49%

Conversion rate

4.2/5

User rating

Impact

O TOTVS Fast Check-in became one of the most valued features of TOTVS PMS, adopted by major hotel chains and significantly improving the experience for both guests and staff.

Key Takeaways

  • Data driven decisions – Analytics (DataDog) were crucial for prioritizing improvements.
  • Design is not just about looks – Integration with PMS and cross-platform experience were essential for success.
  • Measurable impact – The solution not only improved UX but also generated operational savings for hotels.
