Quica e pega
Ambos&&

Interactive installation by the company Ambos&&, produced for an event at Sesc Tijuca celebrating the 201st anniversary of the National Museum, with the theme "Physics in Everyday Life."

Coded in Processing, several balls fall behaving according to each of their Physics, such as elasticity and weight, onto the silhouettes of visitors, captured via a Kinect. Each ball has arrows representing its weight force and speed for educational purposes.
Ambos&& Team: Bárbara Castro, Jacqueline Pires, Júlia Fonseca, Maria Clara Ruback
